Support / Policies

Returns & Refunds

We want you to shop with confidence. If something isn’t right, here’s how returns, exchanges, and refunds work at Kitenge Bora.

24h reporting window Refunds via Mobile Money Delivery fee non-refundable

Eligibility

  • Item is unused, clean and in original condition.
  • Report issues within 24 hours of receiving your order (Kigali).
  • Upcountry: report on the same day the parcel is delivered.

Not returnable

  • Cut or tailored fabric, custom orders, altered items.
  • Items with signs of wear, stains, perfume, or damage after delivery.

How to request a return

  1. Send a WhatsApp message with order details and clear photos.
  2. Tell us whether you want an exchange or refund.
  3. We confirm next steps (pick-up / drop-off / exchange options).

Refunds & exchanges

  • Refunds processed via Mobile Money after inspection.
  • Exchange for another product (top-up difference if needed).
  • If an item is out of stock, we’ll offer alternatives or refund.

Damaged / incorrect items

If you receive a damaged item or a wrong product, contact us immediately with photos. We will prioritise a replacement, exchange, or refund.
